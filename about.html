About

Hey, I'm a 22 year-old french student in Computer Science at INSA-Lyon

I'm especially interested in image processing, procedural generation, and everything leading to more intelligent or aesthetic applications.
Using maths to give sense to raw information, or birth to whole sceneries, is the kind of magic which never grows old to me.

Even though I usually work on desktop projects, I made the Web my playground, enjoying the current exhuberance around the new technologies. I'm also keen on UI design.

I like learning new stuff through personal experiments (hence this website for instance) or group projects (side-projects, game-jams,...).

I enjoy travelling around, seeing new horizons, meeting new people. I'm currently doing an pretty cool internship in Japan, as a gap year.

AFK, I'm a movie buff, a photo hobbyist, a music enthousiast and an occasional mountains roamer.
