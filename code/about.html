Hey, I'm a 25 year-old PhD student in Computer Science at Universität Passau and Siemens CT. I also love photography.

I'm especially interested in visual computing, procedural generation, and everything leading to more intelligent or aesthetic applications.

Even though I usually work on computer vision research projects, I made the Web my playground, enjoying the current exuberance around the new technologies. I'm also keen on UI design.

I like learning new stuff through personal experiments (hence this website) or group projects (side-projects, game-jams,...).

I enjoy travelling around, seeing new horizons, meeting new people. I'm currently based in Munich, enjoying Central Europe and further.

AFK, I'm a movie buff, a photo hobbyist, a music enthusiast and an occasional mountains roamer.
